Nigerian Culture and Heritage

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Site Name: Nigerian Culture and Heritage

Reason: I chose this name because it clearly reflects the purpose of my website — to showcase Nigeria’s rich traditions, modern achievements, and unique cultural identity. It represents pride, unity, and education about our heritage.

Optional domain: nigeriancultureheritage.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to educate and inspire both Nigerians and people worldwide about Nigeria’s culture and heritage. It will feature pages on history, languages, traditional foods, festivals, music, sports, and modern entertainment. The site will serve as a cultural hub for learning and appreciation.

Scenarios

  • What are the most popular traditional festivals celebrated across Nigeria?
  • Where can I learn about the different languages spoken in Nigeria?
  • Which Nigerian foods are most famous internationally?

Color Schema

The site will use the following colors inspired by the Nigerian flag and earthy tones representing heritage:

  • Green (#008751): Used for headings, navigation background, and buttons.
  • White (#ffffff): Used for the main background for clarity and simplicity.
  • Gold (#C5A236): Accent color for borders, highlights, and hover effects.

Typography

  • Heading Font: Merriweather — used for all headings to convey elegance and tradition.
  • Body Font: Roboto — used for paragraphs, lists, and general text for readability.
